Running Wild

When the sea takes everything, the jungle gives him a chance to survive. Swept away by a tsunami and carried into the rainforest on the back of an elephant, Will must learn to trust the wild — and himself — to find his way home.

Scythe

Neal Shusterman

In a world without death, someone has to take life. Chosen to apprentice as Scythes — society’s only sanctioned killers — Citra and Rowan must learn the art of death... or be claimed by it themselves.

Alex Rider

Anthony Horowitz

When MI6 recognises his potential, fourteen-year-old Alex Rider is forcibly recruited, armed with secret gadgets and sent on his first mission. But will he survive? He’s soon in mortal danger, and there’s no turning back.

A Tale Of Two Cities

Charles Dickens

Two cities. One revolution. A story of love, sacrifice, and redemption. In a world on the brink of chaos, a quiet hero must risk everything to save the woman he loves — even if it costs him his life.
